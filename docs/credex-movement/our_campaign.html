Our Campaign

Vision

Our multi-planetary, multi-dimensional, and spiritually mature human civilization possesses incredible wealth, with sustainable economic growth projected as far as the eye can see, in harmony with the biosphere of our Earth.

Our governance and justice systems represent all equally. These systems form our common social bedrock, and they mostly stay out of our way as we live the lives of free, creative, thriving human beings.

Purpose

Establish, protect and empower a primordially independent power centre that is loyal to the principles of human sovereignty and can exist in long-term harmony with the natural world.

Grand Strategy

Act from an ethical and morally powerful centre that energizes us and our allies, magnetizes the uncommitted, and brings potential competitors and adversaries into the circle of our purpose. Overcome challenges and adversaries while respecting every human and taking full moral responsibility for our actions.

Operationalize sovereignty into wealth-producing, biosphere-nurturing, freedom-protecting protocols that enable humans to connect in peer-to-peer, counterparty-to-counterparty, citizen-to-citizen, human-to-human relationships.

Cultivate personal loyalty to human sovereignty and the protocols that enable it.

Strategic Aim

Shape the pursuit of our purpose so as to amplify our spirit, inspiration, and strength, and protect our moral, mental, and physical cohesion. Isolate and solidify challengers and adversaries into fixed positions that can be understood and integrated, or bypassed, as we build momentum towards our purpose. Maintain fluidity and strategic initiative from a place of ethical and moral strength.

Cocreate a global culture of universal sovereignty. Establish patterns that prioritize what matters to humans at a grassroots level, applying systemic moral pressure across a broad front, naturally exposing and undermining adversaries of our purpose, and creating a protected moral space from which to cultivate, enhance, and share our strength.

Strategy

Demonstrate what is possible. Live it. Share it. Protect it. Nurture it. Celebrate it.

Efficient peer-to-peer economic relationships that build mutual wealth independent of outside sovereigns. Entrepreneurial profit. The accumulation, investment, and growth of capital. The gifting of wealth for charity, culture, nature, education, art, sport, passion, beauty, love, friendship, family, and everything that inspires generosity. Mutually beneficial, cooperative, corporate, wealth-generating structures. Innovative democratic protocols that enable equality of voice and cultivate collective wisdom. Freedom, diversity, and self-determination as the birthright of all humans.

Grand Tactics

Orient on the essential, observe, and prepare. Align with reality. Engage specific strategic opportunities with intent to transform and capacity to succeed. Meet apparent attacks with openness and understanding, and respond with clarity and commitment, moving into a position of strength. No matter where we find ourselves, sincerely acting out of loyalty to our purpose attracts allies and moves us towards the high ground.

Find resonance with every point of view in order to see deeply into what is. All humans have the sovereign right to question, criticize, oppose, and resist our agenda. Actively expressing this right is cause for respect. We respect ourselves, our tribe, our comrades, our allies, our counterparties, our observers, our critics, our adversaries, our enemies, and those who don't give a shit. Gracious and humble in victory, in defeat, and in ongoing relationship, our respect shows us the landscape of what truly is.

Tactical Aim

Engage with integrity to initiate patterns that uplift situations towards our purpose. Maintain the tactical initiative from a position of operational strength. Expose and understand challenges.

Risk little to gain much. Risk much to gain everything. Know what matters most, and protect it at all costs.

Protect sovereign humans. Protect our relationships to each other and to our habitat. Weaponize true moral strength and brush away moralism, greenwashing, and virtue-signalling. Expose, utilize, and distribute real power.

Build real wealth for humans. Build sustainable multigenerational prosperity. Support humans to participate in their chosen connections, networks, relationships and structures.

Tactics

Establish and strengthen relationships and increase shared information, emotions, and ideas. Understand the surface pain and the layered pain. Demonstrate our ability to address crucial issues and overcome challenges. Move fluidly into opportunities. Take what is offered. Add value with empathy, clarity, commitment, and effectiveness. Be open and be decisive.

Establish, protect, and empower a digital ecosystem that enables simple, transparent, secure relationships and lines of communication between sovereign humans. Energize organic structures and processes that support the ecosystem's growth and evolution towards stable symbiotic maturity.
