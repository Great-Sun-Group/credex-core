Glossary of Terms

Credcoin (CXX)

The denomination providing the primary reference point for the organic economy

Credex

A smart contract between counterparties that can be used to replace money

Credex Ecosystem

The digital ecosystem in which credexes exist and can operate

Credloop

A loop of payable and receivable credexes that can be cleared against each other

Daily Credcoin Offering (DCO)

The daily process that establishes the relative values of the denominations in the ecosystem

Demurrage

The opposite of interest, implemented in the credex ecosystem as a default

Human Sovereigntism

The belief that free humans are the primary political reference point of global society

Organic Economy

The human economy operating in abundant harmony with our habitat
