Credcoin: Foundations & DCO

Sovereign natural resources

Our earth is bountiful, and finite. Our ecosystem is resilient, and fragile. We are capable of destroying our habitat, and we've done a lot of that. We are also capable of nurturing our habitat, and we've done a lot of that as well.

The impulse for human sovereignty cannot be separated from the impulse to sustain humanity, civilization, and the biosphere for the long term. We belong here, and we are responsible for our actions. We respect the sovereignty of future generations of humanity and we value the natural world in itself. Destroying our habitat at scale is simply not an option. So let's stop. It may be easier than we think.

Let's take personal responsibility for this Earth that we belong to, and that belongs to us. Our purpose is to sustain a bountiful biosphere and human economy in the present and into the indefinite future.

The truth about limits

The starting point is simply to acknowledge the truth that there are limits to the natural resources that can be sustainably harvested from the earth and the biosphere, and there are limits to the wastes that can be sustainably released. Infinite growth in resource consumption is a non-negotiable impossibility.

Our earth is bountiful and has nurtured us since our primal origins. Natural resources are limited, but within these limits exists a sustainable harvest, and within our economy are flows, processes, patterns, and practices occurring in sustainable balance with the natural world at a fundamental level. There are literally infinite wealth-creating opportunities within these sustainable flows. Endless economic growth is possible, but only if we stabilize on a sustainable resource harvest.

It is critically important that we separate the non-negotiable fact that there are limits to sustainable resource harvests, from all opinions about what those limits should be, or who should decide. Reasonable people will differ. Facing those differences can come later. A shared starting point is far more important. And the farther we can extend together from that shared starting point, the stronger will be our chances of overcoming the differences when they arise.

Growth within limits

Our shared starting point here is an acknowledgement that although we can have perpetual growth in the circulation of economic value, we cannot have perpetual growth in resource consumption. The latter is a physical and logical impossibility. These are the truths that must be faced, and we have to take personal economic responsibility for facing them.

In fact, perpetual economic growth only becomes possible when we stabilize our resource consumption. Perpetual economic growth can only occur within the environment of a healthy and productive biosphere. A devastated biosphere will not support a thriving human economy.

When we take personal responsibility for guiding our own resource consumption towards sustainable limits, we also open the door to personal participation in the infinite economic growth that is possible within those limits. Human creativity, ingenuity, and innovation is limitless, and economic value is always relative. This enables theoretically perpetual growth in the circulation of economic value and economic wealth, but only when harnessed within the limits of sustainability.

Sustainable consumption

We are beings who necessarily consume. But by containing our consumption and waste in some ways, and by nurturing the biosphere, we can actually increase the flow of sustainable human consumption and at the same time we can increase the wealth of life on earth as a whole. With this kind of stewardship, our Earth will reward us with bountiful harvests. Failing at this stewardship, we will sooner or later be rewarded with famines.

Belonging: one equal share

The organic economy is a subset of the global economy in which value circulates in resonance with biological processes. The organic economy is founded on the sustainable global harvest of natural resources, and grows richly from there.

Every day, a sustainable harvest of natural resources enters the organic economy. This sustainable harvest is hidden among many unsustainable practices and products, but it's always there. Like this harvest, the organic economy itself is currently hidden, but always present.

You are a sovereign human who belongs on this earth. Your birthright is one equal share of the natural resources entering the organic economy today.

Responsibility: provide value for consumption

We cannot have sovereignty and belonging without responsibility. We are responsible to provide value for the natural resources that we ourselves consume.

When we provide market value to all in exchange for the natural resources that we use, we bring our personal consumption into the organic economy. When we step into this belonging and responsibility, we take our personal human sovereignty to a far deeper and more potent level. And when we step into this belonging and responsibility, we also tap into the perpetual wealth-creation opportunities of an economy operating in harmony with the biosphere.

This core moral truth is the heartstone of credcoin, of the organic economy, and of the credex ecosystem. This truth is our primary economic reference point.

The Daily Credcoin Offering

The credcoin principle is actualized every 24 hours in the daily credcoin offering (DCO). The daily cadence of the DCO is the heartbeat of the primary circulation system of the organic economy.

Your voluntary participation in the DCO transforms the credcoin principle from moral idea to physical reality. The DCO occurs in the credex ecosystem at midnight UTC, and establishes the relative values of the denominations in the ecosystem for the next 24 hours.

In the DCO, the concept of the worldwide organic economy and the natural resource flows that sustain it becomes a real, measured flow of value among real humans. In the DCO we shift our focus away from "the organic economy" as an abstract concept of a portion of the economy that is invisible around us, and place our attention on a real, measured flow of natural resources.

Starting small

Without this granular focus, we are paralyzed by the vastness and pervasiveness of the problem of unsustainability. But by anchoring on a real and measurable flow of value, however small, the DCO enables us to collectively crystalize and start to measure what we know to be an insignificantly tiny portion of the full organic economy.

This real, actual, measurable organic economy based on real flows of values is insignificantly tiny compared to the fully grown and manifested organic economy in the same way that a redwood seed is insignificantly tiny in comparison to the royal old grandparent of the forest it may become.

We can approach the concrete, measurable flows of the organic economy which is centred in the DCO and founded on credcoin as if it were the seed of the idealized concept of the totality of the organic economic flows across the Earth. If we nurture the seed of the concrete existence of this much larger concept, the seed will grow.

But at the same time, it will never be more perfect or complete than it is right now. A seed is not less of a tree than sapling, which is not less of a tree than a giant. It is not less of a tree, it is just smaller in measure. The seed and the giant both contain the full essence of the tree. Through this lens, we can respect the fact that this organic economy seed will grow to something far vaster than what we measure today, yet we can also know that what we measure today is in its essence no less of the organic economy than it will be when the value measured is vastly greater.

That may sound like some kind of spiritual statement, and I guess in a way it is, but it's actually describing a specific mathematical reality that is embedded in the credcoin principle itself, and its execution in the DCO.

Moral choice

Your voluntary participation in the gift and market portions of the DCO is a personal decision to bring your own consumption within the real, actual, measured seed of the growing organic economy. It's a decision to add some of your personal economic energy into this circulation system. It is a personal decision to advance the values of sovereignty, freedom, sustainability, mutual wealth creation, and economic justice.

The DCO is fundamentally non-coercive, enabling you to embody your values without requiring the rest of the world to change. You stand as an example of what is possible, and you create positive change from that place of strength.

Your participation in the DCO crystalizes the credcoin principle into the concrete value of the credcoin (CXX) denomination within the credex ecosystem, onto which all other values are anchored.

How it works

In the DCO, each participant's responsibility to provide value for resource consumption is enacted starting in very simple ways. This value provided by participants establishes the total value of the natural wealth entering the (measured) organic economy today. By dividing this amount by the number of DCO participants, we establish the value of 1.000 CXX for the next 24 hours. The DCO strategically and effectively processes the payments enacted by these calculations in currency, credex, and credcoin.

As a DCO participant, every day you fulfill your responsibility to provide value to all for your consumption of natural resources, and 1.000 CXX is deposited to your credex account. This DCO credex delivers your daily birthright claim to one equal share of the natural wealth entering the organic economy today.

If you consume more than one equal share of natural resources, your higher consumption is balanced and fairly compensated for when you distribute more value to all in the DCO than you receive in your 1.000 CXX deposit. If you consume less than one equal share of resources, the 1.000 CXX deposit to your account is of greater value than what you distribute to all through the DCO due to your consumption.

Gift and market

The total value of the natural wealth entering the organic economy is separated into two components: gift DCO and market DCO. The gift DCO taps into the primal generosity of humans to advance the causes of sovereignty, freedom and sustainability. The market DCO channels the primal self-centeredness of humans towards exactly the same ends, which ennobles price-based decision making across the entire organic economy by incorporating the full costs of natural resources.

Initialization

The DCO transfers real economic values (currency), and promises to provide real economic values (credexes), and it measures these flows of value. With these measurements, the credcoin equation can be solved for the day, linking the denomination of credcoin to currency values with a daily exchange rate. Using that exchange rate, all prices anywhere can be expressed in credcoin.

To initialize the flow of value and solve the equation in the first DCO, the value of 1.000 CXX had to be set arbitrarily, and was pegged to $1.00 CAD (Canadian dollars) on ecosystem launch.

Midnight UTC was chosen as a temporal daily reference point for the DCO because it lies between close of business on one day and opening of business on the next across the majority of the population centres of the world, while the sun is high in the sky over the vast Pacific Ocean.

Since ecosystem launch, the value of CXX has floated based fully on DCO participant gifts, to which market DCO values will eventually be added.
