Credcoin

What is Credcoin?

Credcoin (CXX) is the foundational measure of value within the credex ecosystem. Credcoin is a simple mathematical construct that translates a specific flow of natural resources (and changes in that flow) into a uniquely solid financial anchor point. Transactions in the credex ecosystem can be denominated in credcoin (CXX), and this denomination behaves in unique but predictable ways, inflationlessly anchored to a specific, real, and sustainable flow of natural resources.

Credcoin vs money

Credcoin (CXX) is a denomination that exists within the credex ecosystem and relies on its unique characteristics, anchoring every value in the ecosystem to the value of a real flow of natural wealth. Credex smart contracts could theoretically be implemented programmatically anchored in any currency or measure of value with reliable market rate data, or by enabling native behavior in every currency and adding an alternate implementation of exchange rate processes. However, we have chosen to establish CXX and utilize it as our base reference point because of its strengths:

  1. Credcoin is connected to a real and sustainable flow of natural resources, anchoring our financial tokens to a specific and transparent economic reality and decoupling them from the fluctuations of currency denominations.
  2. Credcoin offers stability of contracted value regardless of future fluctuations in the value of the flow of natural resources.
  3. Credcoin is an implementation of the Credcoin Principle (below), which we believe arises from and speaks to potent human impulses for self-sovereignty and sustainability.
  4. The principles behind this simple formula based denomination may be able to inspire a generation of innovation in formula and derivative based denominations within the credex ecosystem, perhaps opening evolutionary possibilities based on innovations that cannot now be even imagined.
  5. A single computational and conceptual anchor point for valuations has many additional practical, strategic, and systemic advantages.

We are unaware of any prior attempt at anything like credcoin, though the mathematics are simple and we believe it embodies principles that have been explored by many.

Organic economy

The organic economy is a subset of the global economy in which value is produced, circulated and consumed and in which capital is grown, invested and gifted, in harmony with the principles and processes of the natural world. We view the organic economy as a subset of the existing economy. But it currently exists only as a force and a potentiality. With credcoin we desire to cultivate and grow it from potential to embodied reality. The credex ecosystem is designed to bring the organic economy to our consciousness, enable its flows, and facilitate its growth.

Further exploration of the organic economy is beyond the scope of this document, but its existence must be posited in order to understand the principle that lies at the heart of credcoin and therefore at the heart of the credex ecosystem.

Credcoin Principle

Every human being is entitled to one equal share of the natural wealth entering the organic economy.

Every human being is responsible to provide value equal to the amount of natural wealth that they consume.

The credcoin principle can be translated into an equation, and solving the equation every 24 hours in the voluntary daily credcoin offering (DCO) gives us the daily value of 1.000 CXX vs currencies and vs the prior day. The value of one credcoin (1.000 CXX) is one equal share of the natural wealth entering the organic economy today.

Decoupling

CXX is fully mathematical self-referential, just like modern money. This means that the two could exist with no relationship to each other. It is possible to envision a self contained CXX economy that has no exchange rate reference point to currency. Each of these mediums of exchange forms a mathematical system that can theoretically exist without the other, in a similar way that different denominations co-exist within the modern money system. Each theoretically could be run independently. The self-referential independence of CXX decouples it from modern money. This decoupling feature adds a deep layer of resiliency to the entire credex ecosystem.

From an investment point of view, this decoupling is a de-correlation of risk, which means that an investment in the organic economy is naturally hedged to some degree against systemic monetary dislocations. This feature could be used to offer smart contract solutions to countless investment markets. This de-correlation of risk is also an "operational hedge" for businesses and individuals, as it could provide a stream of income and temporary store of value that is unaffected (or less affected) by inflation and other monetary dislocations.

Every 24 hours, the DCO anchors the self-referential nature of credcoin firmly to the underlying economic, ecological and human reality. In this way, credcoin offers the unlimited leverage potential of the credex ecosystem, yet remains grounded in the natural world and real flows of value. Credcoin is decoupled from money and bound tightly to real value.

Mindshift

Perhaps the most profound, powerful, and hard to predict impact of an organic economy where value circulates aided by smart contracts denominated in CXX are the psychological impacts and potential for system wide behaviour changes.

Receiving one daily share of the natural wealth entering the organic economy and viewing all prices relative to that unit has a potentially transformative impact, especially if participants know that the sellers offering these prices have purchased the rights to utilize the natural resources consumed in their business, and the proceeds have been distributed to all.

We believe that there is a potential positive self-referential feedback loop of mindshift, leading to behaviour shift, leading to greater mindshift, that opens completely new horizons of possibility for multigenerational prosperity.

DCO gifts

With the approach indicated above, the formation of specific DCO linked markets becomes possible. Their implementation and unification at scale is a massive project, perhaps unlike anything ever attempted, but launching the organic economy and the DCO does not depend on scale. It depends on participation, and a single participant is mathematically enough for continuity.

The gift portion of the DCO achieves two purposes. First, it enables an easier access point into the organic economy. A single human, or a handful of people can bring the organic economy to life with their participation in the DCO, and that participation only requires the giving of a small and low friction daily gift. These gifts form part of the DCO pool that is distributed as 1.000 CXX to all participants.

With this mechanism, we strategically drop the systemic barrier to ecosystem launch to the lowest possible level, and align the organic economy with the deepest impulses of participants. If human beings want the organic economy to exist, we will breath life into it through our gifts, then we will build and strengthen the market infrastructure to scale it.

The second purpose of the gift portion of the DCO is to mathematically link the value of CXX to all currency and market values. The market portion of the DCO occurs completely in CXX, creating the self-referential resilience of the ecosystem. The gift portion of the DCO includes a gift of currency, and in so doing anchors the CXX exchange rates vs currency and all global markets.

As gift participants in the DCO, we are gifting some of the value we have accumulated in the money economy into the organic economy in a very specific and generative way. We see this as moving the first small piece of our resource consumption into the organic economy. In doing this through the DCO, we distribute this value equally to all. In return we receive our own equal portion of the total value, and open our door to the organic economy. Here we can start to actively grow our own portion of the sustainable, multigenerational wealth that the credex ecosystem is designed to help each of us build.

Organic economy certification

The creation of DCO markets will go hand in hand with organic economy certification ratings providing information about the degree of organic economy activity embedded into every transaction. By participating in the DCO and accountably embedding organic economy values into the prices of their products and services, businesses will be able to access new markets made up of other nodes who also want to bring their consumption more into line with ecological principles, environmental sustainability, freedom, and self-sovereignty.

Regulation

Certification processes can be incredibly successful based on optional participation, whereas government regulations enforce participation and insist on 100% compliance. If you do not have a government approved commercial fishing license, you will be prevented from harvesting fish to sell.

The organic economy and the resource flows entering it could be linked to government regulation and legislation this way. Some governments might be resistant to the principle of sharing the value of the natural resources harvested in their jurisdiction equally with all DCO participants rather than balancing their own budgets or distributing the value only to their own citizens. This resistance would be tempered by the equivalent flows of value that would flow into a jurisdiction from the participation in other regions. If this resistance could be overcome or accommodations made within the DCO process, governments could potentially utilize the DCO in their own policymaking, under conditions that benefit governments, their citizens, and members, and the whole ecosystem.

The credex ecosystem, credex, credcoin, and the organic economy do not require this kind of government involvement to function, but in the future the two could conceivably be linked.

Embed sustainability into prices

When businesses pay for the sustainable flow of natural resources that they require to deliver their products and services, their prices begin to have sustainability baked in. In this context, a premium will gradually start to build in the organic economy on products and services that consume more natural resources, with the value of that premium distributed to all DCO participants equally. In this way, value flows and capital creation is intentionally guided down the paths that require the fewest resources, as naturally as water flowing downhill. In this way, the organic economy integrates the forces of markets and capital with ecological forces and realities, and enables ecology and economy to grow wealth in reciprocal harmony. In this way, we make the sustainability premium something we can be proud to pay, knowing that it goes to all equally, directly, and daily, and that those who need it most receive the most benefit from this generative pattern.

Poverty alleviation

In this way, we offer each human being the birthright of at least a minimal flow of wealth to support basic human existence, and we offer the opportunity to cooperate directly with others to grow that flow into a pool, a stream, a river, or an ocean.

Implemented at global scale, the DCO has the potential to alleviate the most extreme forms of poverty and raise the floor for material human existence everywhere. Money has nothing comparable.

The DCO could even be seen as a unique implementation of the principles of Universal Basic Income (UBI). Credcoin embeds the principles of human dignity and economic justice directly into our very medium of exchange, right alongside the values of sustainability and mutual prosperity. Furthermore, the DCO achieves all this in a way that provides an incomparably rock solid economic anchor point, rather than the economic distortion that critics of other UBI proposals fear.

Values

Voluntary participation of members and their businesses in the DCO centres the credex ecosystem and especially the denomination of credcoin firmly on the values of respect, freedom, diversity, sustainability, unity, personal sovereignty, and mutual prosperity.

The only core value that could be said to be similarly implemented in the rulesets of the money system is the value of individual prosperity via competitive profit that is embedded in the debt overhang of money economies.

The credex ecosystem brings to life deep human values that money simply has no relationship with. Comparing credcoin to money with regards to the values embodied in their systemic rulesets leads to a clear conclusion. Credcoin is a medium of exchange and modern money is a medium of exchange, but credcoin is better in a deeply human-centric and earth-centric way.
