Credex Ecosystem Services

Executive Summary

The services detailed below and presented throughout this documentation provide a complete suite of functionality with the potential to scale to manage resource and value flows across the global economy. This functionality has an independent, cryptographically secured existence, locked to the authorization unit of the sovereign human.

The credex ecosystem natively exceeds the compliance standards of all jurisdictions with low-friction user experiences, enabling its adoption in any environment and opening opportunities for direct, organic, peer-to-peer, networked financial relationships from anywhere to anywhere, via anywhere in between.

  • Digital Identity: Human-centric data structure, with all data ultimately locked cryptographically and biologically to our sovereign human members.
  • Data: Ability to create, structure, reference, and secure any data, with native sharing and co-creating permissions.
  • Corporations: Any corporate, cooperative, for-profit, non-profit, or trust, structured as relationships between members, and able to act in simular ways to members.
  • Avatars: Programmatic authorization of contracts, for example recurring payments.
  • Accounts: Owned by member or corporation, able to issue credexes and transact in the credex ecosystem.
  • Transactions: Credexes at their most simple. Income and expense entries between two accounts, with corresponding contingent liability/asset entries and transfer of asset ownership between members/corporations.
  • Assets: Ownership tokens for any kind of asset, including extensive data about the asset.
  • Liabilities: Debts between accounts, on any terms.
  • Credit History: Independently verifiable and selectively sharable accounting entries provide credit histories that are owned by the creditor but can provide third-party verified assurance of past performance to the lender.
  • Equity: Fractional ownership of corporations.
  • Bonds: First (and tiered) claims on cashflows and assets of a corporation.
  • Gifts: To support any cause.

Digital Identity and Personal Data

  • Secure and democratically-protected digital human identity management.
  • Initially linked to government issued ID with facial recognition.
  • Additional biomarker data to be added in the future in order to securely, reliably, and verifiably link one human to one member account in the credex ecosystem.
  • Enable various levels of "is a real human" confirmation between members, up to and including sharing any personal information that is requested and approved.
  • Native permission structures for storing and securely sharing data of any kind.

Avatars, Corporations, and Accounts

  • Delegation and/or programmatic authorization (called an "avatar") that is able to sign on behalf of a human or group of humans, with initial functionality built for the creation and approval of recurring payments.
  • Corporations implemented as a specific type of avatar, collectively controlled through a shareholder agreement and authorized representatives.
  • Secure registration of corporations and associations under any kind of relationship desired by the owners, members, or founders.
  • Accounts that can be owned by any human or corporation, and connected in any relationship schema.
  • By default every account can transact with every other account across the ecosystem, with optional permissions to restrict accounts to in-company transactions, mimicking the behaviour of traditional isolated accounting software within a private integrated subset of the shared ledger.

Transaction Contracts

  • Multi-denominational payment processing, banking, and automated bookkeeping.
  • Transaction contracts denominated in currency.
  • Transaction contracts denominated in anything with a daily or periodically verifiable market price (commodity, bond, equity, cryptocurrency, real estate, specific art piece, etc).
  • Contracts linked to specific programmatic triggers within economic processes, as agreed by counterparties.

Financialization

  • Transaction contracts anchored to any formula-based value structure, index, derivative, combination, or conditional statement.
  • Contracts secured or partially secured by real assets.
  • Contracts in inflationless denominations.
  • Contracts denominated in credcoin.

Assets

  • Digital registration, management, and exchange of real assets.

Multiparty Chains and Dynamic Balance Sheets

  • Transparent and private data chains tracking real assets as they flow through the business processes of multiple corporations on their way to final consumption.
  • Transparent and private data chains tracking liabilities across the financial system for highly efficient risk analysis and stress testing.
  • Dynamically constructed and granularly auditable balance sheets for all entities.

Accounting & Compliance

  • Multi-denominational accounting and reporting, with single-denominational exports available.
  • Auditable trails including digital signatures.
  • Granular risk analysis.

Funding

  • Debt secured by assets.
  • Debt secured by general credit (bonds).
  • Equity.
  • Unsecured debt.
  • Financial innovation and formula-based funding contracts using the above paramaters and others.

Investing and diversification

  • Direct peer-to-peer access to companies seeking funding.
  • Contribute to investment pools that invest under any criteria.
  • Securitize, leverage, hedge, and tranche investments as desired with counterparties anywhere, with granularly auditable trails for risk analysis.
  • Diversify wealth from any owned asset through fractional divestiture.

Exiting

  • Corporate and ownership records that are a single reliable source of truth and that can be smoothly and securely transferred or fractionalized under any agreed conditions.

Gifting

  • Direct distribution of accumulated economic value to your chosen causes anywhere.
